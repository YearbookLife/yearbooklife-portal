Knowledge Base

How do I submit my yearbook for printing?
Submission instructions vary by software. Visit the "Quick Links" tab to find specific submission guides for Pictavo, YBLive, or Canva. Generally, you'll need to finalize your design, run a preflight check, and export according to our specifications.
When will I receive my proof?
Digital proofs are typically sent within 3-5 business days after you submit your final files. You'll have 3-5 business days to review and approve or request changes before we proceed to printing.
Can I add more books to my order?
Yes! Use the "Request Spec Change" tab to submit a request to increase your book quantity. Please note that changes may affect your delivery date and total pricing. Our team will confirm all details before processing.
What photo resolution do you require?
For best print quality, photos should be at least 300 DPI and minimum 2MB in file size. Your design software will alert you if images are too low resolution. Avoid downloading photos from social media platforms.
How do I set up my online storefront?
Visit the "Quick Links" tab and click "Set Up Your Storefront" to access the information sheet. Complete the form with your school details, and our team will set up your personalized online store within 2-3 business days.
